e-GRAS Mobile is an Android front-end application designed to interact with e-GRAS(Electronic Government Receipt and Accounting System), which is a government's electronic payment gateway to receive payments online. This app facilitates online tax payment and challan generation process right from Android phones. Besides, the app let the users review their transaction history and search for already generated challans.
