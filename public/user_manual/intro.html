e-GRAS Mobile is an android application designed to interact with e-GRAS(Electronic Government Receipt and Accounting System), which is a government's electronic payment gateway to receive payments online. This app facilitates online tax payment and challan generation process right from Android phones. Besides, the app lets users review their transaction history and search for already generated challans.

The Home Screen of the application displays a list of transactions made recently by the user. The transactions are organized in reverse chronological order.

The User Navigatioin Menu slides in from the left side of the app. All the functionalities of the app are arranged neatly inside the nav menu.
